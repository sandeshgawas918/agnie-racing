10th GKDC - AIR 3

Winner - Engineering Design

We emerged victorious in a fierce war amongst sixty three other engineering designs. Agnie Racing bagged the award for the best engineering design in 10th GKDC by a stupendous margin. Speeding to the finish line with a machine on par industrial standards is what we do. .

Winner - Innovation Award

Agnie racing emerged as the trailblazers of innovation in the 10th GKDC, we set the standards through precise, yet out of the box engineering. Our team integrated 4 ingenious systems into our vehicle and bagged the award for best Innovations amongst 63 other teams

Winner - Cost Event

We proved that engineering brilliance needn't be cost intensive. We built the best designed vehicle, put in various innovations yet managed to make the most affordable car. Sounds unachievable right? Well, we are Agnie Racing.

Winner - Best Female Participant

We participated in Prestigious Go-Kart Design Challenge Conducted by Indian Society of New Era Engineers (ISNEE). In the Event held at Kari Motor Speedway, Coimbatore, Team has shown commendable performance and won several awards including First Female Driver in the history of GKDC.

Runner up - Endurance

The endurance event in GKDC is a grueling test of both the vehicle's capabilities and the team's strategic decision-making. It requires the cars to complete a set number of laps around a demanding track, pushing the limits of performance, reliability, and efficiency. achieving runner up in this was the ultimate test of our engineering prowess and ability to adapt to real-world scenarios.
