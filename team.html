Women in Motorsports

FIRST FEMALE DRIVER IN THE HISTORY OF GKDC

Ms.Chandana Kashyap Is a part of Agnie Racing from past 3.5 Years. We are Proud to announce that she is the first female driver in the history of GKDC. In 10th GKDC, she has shown tremendous ability and consistency and lead the team forward by cleraning Brakes and Accelaration test in the very first attempt.

BEST FEMALE PARTICIPANT-10th GKDC

Our Team is proud to announce Ms.Chandana Kashyap has won the best female participant award of 10th GKDC along with the recognition of first female driver in the history of GKDC. Apart from being a driver, she is actively leading the static team from past two years and contributed to teams effort in reaching heights.
